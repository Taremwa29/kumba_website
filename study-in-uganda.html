Study in Uganda

Your pathway to quality higher education in Uganda

An education placement and student support program under Kumba Education Center, created to help students and professionals access quality higher education opportunities in Uganda.

Why Uganda

One of East Africa's leading education destinations

Uganda offers affordable tuition, recognized academic programmes, diverse institutions, and a supportive learning environment for both local and international students.

Yet many students face challenges choosing the right course, identifying suitable institutions, understanding admission requirements, and navigating the transition into university life. Study in Uganda exists to make that journey easier.

We provide professional guidance, university placement, admissions support, and student transition services — whether you're pursuing a diploma, degree, postgraduate qualification, or professional advancement.

Our Services

Support from application to settlement

University & College Placement

We help students identify and apply to suitable universities and colleges in Uganda based on their academic background, career interests, and financial ability. Our support includes course selection, institution matching, admission application support, entry requirement guidance, and follow-up with institutions.

Career Guidance & Academic Advisory

We provide personalized academic and career guidance to help students make informed choices about their future.

Admissions Documentation Support

We assist students in preparing and organizing the required application documents to improve admission success.

International Student Transition Support

For students coming to Uganda from other countries, we provide support in admission processing, visa guidance, travel preparation, accommodation orientation, settlement support, and university onboarding guidance.

Postgraduate Placement Support

We support professionals seeking diploma upgrades, bachelor's degrees, master's programmes, and PhD opportunities in Uganda.

Institutional Linkages

Through our network of partner universities and colleges, we connect students to trusted and recognized institutions across Uganda.
