Research Support

The evidence your organisation needs to make better decisions

We help organisations of every level gather, analyse, and understand the information they need to run and improve their operations — from needs assessments and baseline studies to evaluations and operational research. We also support students carrying out research at advanced education levels.

Our Focus

Research built around organisational decisions

Most organisations don't lack ambition — they lack the right information at the right time. We work with institutions to answer the practical questions behind their operations: what's working, what isn't, what their clients need, and where to invest next.

Through our specialist-hub model, we assemble experienced researchers and analysts across disciplines to design studies, collect and analyse data, and turn it into clear, actionable evidence you can act on.

We also support students, universities, and individual researchers at advanced education levels — though strengthening organisations is where we focus most.

Our Core Service Areas

Support at every stage of research

Organisational & Operational Research

We answer the questions behind your operations — needs assessments, baseline and market studies, client and beneficiary feedback, and operational research that informs planning, strategy, and day-to-day decisions.

Data Analysis & Interpretation

We turn raw data into clear, accurate, and actionable insight — using industry-standard tools for both quantitative and qualitative analysis so you can act with confidence.

Monitoring, Evaluation & Learning (MEAL)

We design baseline studies, evaluations, impact assessments, and learning systems that show what your programmes are achieving and where they can improve.

Grants & Proposal Development

We help institutions and individuals identify funding opportunities, develop competitive proposals, prepare donor-compliant budgets, and manage grant implementation.

Research Capacity Building

We build in-house research skills through practical training in research methods, data management, evidence-based decision-making, and reporting — so your team can sustain the work themselves.

Student & Academic Research Support

Alongside our organisational work, we guide students at advanced education levels through proposals, dissertations, and theses — from methodology and data analysis to academic writing and publication.

Research Systems Strengthening

Building sustainable research ecosystems
